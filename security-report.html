Cybersecurity Annual Report 2026

Published by the International Cybersecurity Council

Cyber threats evolved significantly in 2025-2026, with AI-powered attacks becoming the primary concern for organizations worldwide.

Top Threats

  1. AI-generated phishing (up 450% YoY)
  2. Supply chain attacks targeting open-source packages
  3. Ransomware-as-a-Service platforms
  4. Cloud configuration vulnerabilities
  5. Deepfake-based social engineering

Defense Recommendations

Organizations should implement zero-trust architectures, enhance employee security awareness training, and adopt AI-powered threat detection systems.

The average cost of a data breach reached $5.2 million in 2025, a 12% increase from the previous year.

Budget Trends

Global cybersecurity spending is expected to reach $280 billion in 2026, with the fastest growth in AI security tools and cloud-native protection platforms.
